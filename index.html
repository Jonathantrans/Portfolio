Mask or Die

Live Demo Â· Devpost Website

Mask or Die is a game development project submitted to SacHacks, a Sacramento Hackathon where there are time constraints of 48 hours to design and code. My team and I utilized C# and Unity to code the mechanics of the game, creating a first-person, mask-shooting game as a non-profit PPE for the Sacramento community in order to raise awareness of COVID-19 cases. Google Maps SDK was used to import the map of Sacramento into the game, so the player would spawn right outside of the capital building. As the player walks around in the game, the Google Maps SDK will build the streets of Sacramento around you, looking exactly as it does in the 3D view of Google Maps. In addition, we created a circuit diagram, seen in the picture above, for an immersive experience through a mask.
